ABOUT ME

I'm a busy mother of 4 children and originally from the USA. I would love to inspire many other women and mothers to give Zumba Fitness a go!

Many women have said to me, I would love to give it a try, but I'm too afraid, or I don't how to dance. Here's your chance to give it a go!

My class will be perfect for those beginners, but it will also be a great workout for those that have also done it before.

I have taught from ages 5-75 years old! So no matter what your age, shape or fitness/dance level is, become inspired with me to "Party Your Shape Into Finess"!

If I can do it, so can you!

Its fun, its fitness, its exercise in disguise!
