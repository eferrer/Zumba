Waitakere Weddings presents ...

Auckland's most romantic wedding location, the Waitakere Ranges and Kumeu Valley, for your beach, garden or vineyard wedding ceremony, reception or honeymoon in New Zealand.

Just 30 minutes from Auckland International Airport and the centre of Auckland City, the Waitakere Ranges and Kumeu Valley offers a 40,000-acre wonderland of beaches, ocean views, waterfalls, meandering streams, lush scenic valleys with charming wineries, and exotic gardens.

Here at Waitakere Weddings, we have assembled an easy-to-use wedding directory of wedding celebrants, photographers, bridal salons, florist, wedding venues, caterers and more to meet all your wedding and honeymoon requirements.
